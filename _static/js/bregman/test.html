An interactive visualisation in browsers which support the HTML5 canvas tag and have Javascript enabled.

_Figure 1_: An interactive geometric interpretation of the Bregman divergence. Hover to move $x$, click to place $x'$. The red line is the tangent to $f$ at $x'$. The length of the blue vertical line shows the Bregman divergence $B_f(x,x')$.
